# Greeter

**Commitment length:** 1 Month

## Role Description

The Greeter is the first point of contact for attendees at our civic tech meetups. They ensure a warm welcome and guide participants to the event space.

## Responsibilities

1. **Arrive Early** — Arrive at the venue 15 minutes before the scheduled start time.

2. **Locate Entrance** — Identify the designated entrance for the meetup:
   - **CDH:** Front door
   - **ENAP:** Elevator on the event floor

3. **Position Yourself** — Stand near the entrance to be easily visible to arriving attendees. (Note: A table and chairs are available for sitting at CDH.)

4. **Welcome Attendees** — Greet each attendee with a friendly greeting: "Hello," "Welcome," or "Bonjour."

5. **Direct Attendees** — Politely direct attendees to the meeting space:
   - **At CDH:** Ask them to sign in at the front desk and proceed to the Noviciate on the second floor
   - **At ENAP:** Direct them to the meeting space on the event floor

6. **Answer Basic Questions** — Be prepared to answer questions about restrooms, event flow, and other basic information. If you don't know the answer, direct the person to another organizer.

7. **Maintain a Positive Attitude** — Ensure a welcoming and positive atmosphere for all attendees.

8. **Support Late Arrivals** — Once the event has started, if people are still arriving:
   - Ask them to join quietly in the back
   - Stay by the door for 10–15 minutes after the event starts to direct any late arrivals
