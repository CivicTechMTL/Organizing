# Project Lead

**Commitment length:** Variable (dependent on project duration)

## Role Description

The Project Lead is responsible for guiding and managing a specific civic tech project. They provide direction, foster collaboration, and ensure progress towards the project's goals. This role involves active participation in weekly meetups, where project updates and collaborative work sessions take place, with a strong focus on defining and communicating clear next steps.

*Note: The project lead doesn't need to be the person who started the project—just someone who is consistently involved and committed to the project's success.*

## Responsibilities

1. **Project Announcement** — At the start of project work time:
   - Clearly announce the project's description and goals
   - Specify the skills and expertise needed from potential contributors
   - Outline the tasks planned for the session

2. **Inclusive Invitation** — Invite all attendees to participate, regardless of skill level:
   - Encourage those who wish to learn by observing to join the project space
   - Make it clear that no prior experience is necessary

3. **Facilitate Collaborative Work** — Create an inclusive environment by:
   - Encouraging open communication and group work
   - Creating space for the sharing of ideas and diverse perspectives
   - Supporting team members at all skill levels

4. **Promote Active Participation** — Actively involve team members in the project's tasks by:
   - Encouraging questions
   - Providing clear explanations
   - Delegating work based on interests and abilities

5. **Support Self-Directed Progress** — Recognize that project work is self-led and varies between groups:
   - Help keep the project moving forward
   - Facilitate discussions without micromanaging
   - Adapt to the group's pace and needs

6. **Maintain a Positive Environment**
   - Ensure discussions remain respectful and constructive
   - Help resolve any conflicts or disagreements that may arise
   - Celebrate progress and contributions

7. **Define and Communicate Next Steps** — Before the end of the session:
   - Clearly define the next steps for the project
   - Ensure all team members understand their individual tasks and responsibilities
   - Provide a method of communication between meetings (e.g., Slack, email list, shared document)
   - Create a clear understanding of what will be worked on at the next meetup
