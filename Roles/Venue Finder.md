# Venue Finder

**Commitment length:** 3 Months

## Role Description

The Venue Finder is responsible for securing suitable venues for the weekly civic tech meetups. They research, contact, and scout potential locations, ensuring they meet the group's requirements for accessibility, capacity, and technical needs.

## Responsibilities

1. **Identify Potential Venues** — Research venues that meet our needs:
   - Can accommodate approximately 20 people
   - Located within a 10-minute walk of a metro station
   - Offer free or low-cost usage
   - Are accessible for all attendees

2. **Contact Venues** — Reach out to potential venues:
   - Use email or phone contact
   - Inquire about availability for Wednesday evenings, 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM, for the upcoming month
   - Request information about:
     - Venue capacity
     - Available equipment (screen, projector)
     - Accessibility features

3. **Scout Venues** — Schedule and conduct site visits:
   - Evaluate venue suitability based on:
     - Capacity and layout
     - Availability of necessary equipment
     - Accessibility features
     - Overall atmosphere
     - Proximity to public transit

4. **Organize Venue Bookings** — Secure the venues:
   - Book venues for the entire month
   - Confirm booking details with the venue and organizers
   - Communicate venue details to the organizers and community

5. **Maintain Venue Records** — Keep organized records:
   - Contacted venues and their responses
   - Booking details and confirmations
   - Venue evaluations
   - Create a list of backup venues in case of unforeseen circumstances

## Email Templates

### Initial Venue Inquiry

**Subject:** Venue Inquiry — Civic Tech Montreal Meetups

Dear [Venue Contact Name],

My name is [Your Name], and I'm reaching out on behalf of Civic Tech Montreal. We're the Montreal chapter of the broader civic tech community. At our weekly hacknight events, we aim to create a collaborative environment where people can discuss civic issues and explore technological solutions. No tech background is required to participate, and a multidisciplinary approach is encouraged.

We're looking for a space to hold our hacknights on **Wednesday evenings from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM** for a month. We typically rotate venues each month to share opportunities across the community.

For a better understanding of what a typical hacknight looks like, you can check out the Toronto chapter: https://civictech.ca/

**We need:**
- Capacity for approximately 20 people
- A screen or projector for presentations
- Accessibility features
- Proximity to a metro station (within a 10-minute walk)
- Free or low-cost usage

Would your venue be available for these dates and potentially on a recurring basis? If so, could you please provide information about your venue's capacity, available equipment, and accessibility features? We would also be very interested in scheduling a site visit to evaluate the space.

Thank you for your time and consideration. We look forward to hearing from you!

Sincerely,

[Your Name]  
[Your Contact Information]  
Civic Tech Montreal
