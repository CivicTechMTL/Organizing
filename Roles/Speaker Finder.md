# Speaker Finder

**Commitment length:** 3 Months

## Role Description

The Speaker Finder is responsible for identifying and securing speakers for the weekly civic tech meetups. They research potential speakers, build a contact list, and communicate with speakers to arrange presentations on relevant topics related to civic technology and community work.

## Responsibilities

1. **Build a Speaker List** — Research and identify potential speakers:
   - Look in the civic tech community, local organizations, and educational institutions
   - Prioritize individuals with interesting stories and relevant experience
   - Focus on community engagement over reputation
   - Collect speaker contact information, expertise description, and potential topics

2. **Contact Potential Speakers** — Reach out via email or phone:
   - Introduce Civic Tech Montreal and its mission
   - Briefly explain the meetup format and audience
   - Propose potential speaking topics and dates

3. **Introduction Conversation** — Schedule a brief call (phone or video) to:
   - Discuss the speaker's background and presentation ideas
   - Answer any questions they have
   - Ensure they understand the community-focused nature of the meetups

4. **Maintain Email Communication** — Keep clear and consistent contact:
   - Provide necessary information about venue, date, time, and presentation format
   - Share relevant resources or guidelines
   - Confirm any special needs or requests

5. **Confirm Speaker Attendance** — Send formal confirmation:
   - Include all relevant details in a confirmation email
   - Remind speakers of the date, time, venue, and presentation topic
   - Confirm any technical requirements or equipment needs

6. **Maintain Speaker Records** — Keep organized records:
   - Contacted speakers and communication details
   - Confirmed presentations and dates
   - Speaker feedback and notes for future reference

We believe your experience and insights would be incredibly valuable to our community. We'd love to invite you to speak at one of our upcoming meetups about [suggest a relevant topic]. Our meetups are focused on people who are passionate about using technology to address civic challenges. We're a community-focused group, and we value real stories and practical experiences over formal presentations.

Would you be open to a brief conversation to discuss this opportunity further?

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
