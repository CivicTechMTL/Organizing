# Volunteer Roles

This section outlines all volunteer roles at Civic Tech Montreal. Each role includes detailed responsibilities, commitment length, and step-by-step guidance for success.

## Event Facilitation Roles

### [Greeter](Roles/Greeter.md)
**Commitment**: 1 Month | **Type**: Event Support
First point of contact for attendees. Welcomes people and guides them to the event space.

### [Host](Roles/Host.md) (Emcee)
**Commitment**: 1 Month | **Type**: Event Leadership
Guides the flow of the meetup, facilitates introductions, and manages the event agenda.

### [Civic Tech 101 Host](Roles/Civic Tech 101 Host.md)
**Commitment**: 1 Month | **Type**: Education
Leads a dedicated session introducing newcomers to civic technology concepts and projects.

## Project Leadership

### [Project Lead](Roles/Project Lead.md)
**Commitment**: Variable | **Type**: Project Management
Guides and manages a specific civic tech project, fostering collaboration and progress.

## Logistics & Outreach

### [Venue Finder](Roles/Venue Finder.md)
**Commitment**: 3 Months | **Type**: Logistics
Secures suitable venues for weekly meetups with necessary facilities and accessibility.

### [Speaker Finder](Roles/Speaker Finder.md)
**Commitment**: 3 Months | **Type**: Outreach
Identifies and books speakers for meetup presentations on civic tech topics.

---

## How to Get Started

1. **Choose a role** that matches your interests and availability
2. **Review the runsheet** to understand responsibilities
3. **Contact the current role holder** for a handoff
4. **Refer back** to the runsheet as needed during your commitment
